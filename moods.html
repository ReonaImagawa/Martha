The moods of a wild danpan

Angry: When a wild danpan it's best to lock them in a sealed room. To calm an angry danpan down it's also best to push a few panini's underneath the door and they'll eventually calm down after 5-8 panini's.

Hungry: Feed a danpan as many paninis as you can before she turns into an angry danpan.

Rude: If you danpan is rude take out the belt.
